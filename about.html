About Us

Creating reliable, professional and caring online services to improve

access to mental health care.

Are you struggling with a mental illness and you cant afford to visit

a psychiatric hospital

Talk To Us Now!

By entering our website you step into a safe space in which committed

empathetic psychiatrists provide excellent services.

They will support you and manage your illness at the comfort of

your home through online sessions or refer you to a hospital where they

can physically attend to you.

Our Vision

We aim to enable people of all social classes to afford mental health care and bring

you affordable services with just a click of a button.

Our Mission

When your life is stifled by discomfort ,We come to the rescue. We peel off the layers

of physical restriction through a natural approach to health care

and healthy lifestyle modifications
