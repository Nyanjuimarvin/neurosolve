Our Approach

Creating Reliable, Professional and Caring Online Services to improve access to
mental health care. Are you struggling with a mental illness and you can't afford to visit a psychiatric
hospital? Talk To Us Now!

By entering our website you step into a safe space, in which committed empathetic
psychiatrists to provide excellent services. They will support and help you manage your illness at the comfort of your home through
online sessions or refer you to a hospital they can physically attend to you.

We aim to enable people of all social classes to afford mental health care, hence
bringing you affordable services with just a click of a button.

Neurosolve offers personal, strictly confidential client-orientated video sessions through
the website.
